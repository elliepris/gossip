About The Project

Buzz Battleship reimagines seven classic stories as modern gossip — because beneath every timeless tale is a piece of hot drama that keeps getting retold.

Each story here was originally penned centuries ago by Shakespeare, Austen, Shaw, or their peers, then reborn in the late 1990s and early 2000s as a coming-of-age movie. The DNA of the drama never changed — only the fashion.

The 90s and early 2000s were a golden era for adapting classics into teen films. Clueless, She's All That, 10 Things I Hate About You, Bridget Jones's Diary, Ever After, She's the Man, and Romeo + Juliet all took centuries-old plots and gave them scrunchies, AIM screen names, and pop-punk soundtracks.

This project strips them down further — into the format these stories would take today: unserious group chats. Would you recognize Romeo and Juliet if they showed up as a rant in your DMs? Would Twelfth Night read as drama… or as a cursed Reddit post?

The game asks you to rate each story on two axes: how juicy the gossip is, and how serious it actually is. Then you plot it on the grid. The "correct" answers reflect survey data from anonymous readers.

Play around. Trust your gut. Some of the stories will surprise you.
